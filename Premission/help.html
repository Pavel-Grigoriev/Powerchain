Getting Started

We suggest using Chrome Browser on PC or use Cipher browser for mobile with PowerChain.
After you went to http://powerchain.top in your browser you should have Metamask plugin installed for PC in order to use the power of blockchain technology. After you have downloaded metamask,you must create an account. It will be your access to ETHEREUM BLOCKCHAIN NETWORK. Now you have your own ETHEREUM ADDRESS in the blockhain. PowerChain uses Ropsten Test Network. So, you have to switch from Mainnet to Ropsten in the top right window in Metamask.
Then RELOAD the page.Congratulations! Now your data can be easily stored into the blockchain.

Working with the BLOCKCHAIN through Metamask

When the data is pushed to the blockchain you have to confirm your transacion in Metamask. By clicking the "Подтвердить" button in the plugin. If you do not confirm the transaction the data will no be stored into the blockchain. You will see the following alert "user denied transaction".
You can simply retry passing the transacion if you have denied the previous one or in case there was an error...

Creating an Account in PowerChain

Return to the welcome main page by clicking the HOME button on op of this page. You can notice the JOIN US button on top oh the header. Click on it and you will be redirected to the Registration page. The website reads your Ethereum address automatically. Simply write your username and password, then click the Sign In button.

!!!Attention!!! We strongly recommend taking a username that will be easy for the teacher to indetify. For example, "Pavel Grigoriev". Password has to be strong enough, for example, "Powerchain_top1533". Make sure you do not forget it!

Login System

Before starting using PowerChain you have to log in. Go o the login page by clicking the LOG IN button on the main page. Input you password and click Log In.
Our website uses cookies for authorisation. Cookies expire every 5 minutes. After the cookie expires you log out automatically, then you have to log in manually, to set another cookie.
If you want to log out, simply go to the login page and you will be redirected to the log out page. Then isimply click the Log Out button.

Wallet Usage

After clicking the Wallet button on the main page you will be redirected to the Wallet page. You can see your PWR token balance on top. These tokens can be sent to you by teachers for social activity an etc. or by other students as a donation.
If you want to donate some tokens to another student, you should choose the account in the selector on the page(make sure that the username is correct). Then input the amount of tokens that you want to send and click the SEND button. You must confirm the transacion in Metamask.
Refresh te page in a couple of minutes...
Notice that you can not send more tokens than are on your balance!

Shop Usage

If you want to use the amazing features in our online shop, simply click the Shop button on the main page. Choose the item you want to buy and click Buy. You must confirm the transacion in Metamask.
Notice than you can not spend more tokens that are on your balance!
To get your item you should show the administrator the transaction hash in his metamask account(transfer of PWR tokens) and take the item from the storage!
